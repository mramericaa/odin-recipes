Grandma Colleen's Christmas Meatballs

Description

These meatballs will leave you craving more! Fresh ground beef, sage-flavored sausage, breadcrumbs and more come together for a homey holiday taste

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds Jimmy Dean Sage Sausage
  • 2 Pounds Ground Beef
  • 2 Cups Breadcrumbs
  • 1 Large Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Evaporated Milk
  • Garlic Salt, Onion Salt, and Seasoned Salt to Taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and mix with hands until a uniform consistency is reached
  2. Form meat mix into approximately one-inch diameter balls
  3. Lightly coat a large frying pan with oil and fry balls until golden brown on all sides
  4. Place balls onto a greased baking sheet and bake at 350* for 25 minutes
  5. Plate and enjoy! Best served with brown gravy, but equally as delicious on their own!
